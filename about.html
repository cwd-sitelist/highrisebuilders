About Us

Santhakumar, B.E – Civil Engineer

Make with love
all what we do.

Santhakumar is a passionate civil engineer who began his professional journey in 2021 after completing his Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering. With strong technical knowledge and practical experience in construction, he has successfully completed more than 30 projects as a freelancer over the past three years.

With a vision to deliver quality construction and reliable service, he established his company under the brand name High Rise Builders one year ago. Since then, the company has been involved in the construction of several residential and commercial buildings, focusing on quality workmanship, modern design, and timely project delivery.

Through dedication, expertise, and client trust, High Rise Builders continues to grow as a reliable name in the construction industry.
